MORE THAN JUST PIZZA

We are delighted to present to you Pizzorino, your new favorite pizza destination

At Pizzorino, we aim to transport you to the charming streets of Italy, where the aroma of freshly baked pizza fills the air and every bite is a culinary delight


Pizzorino was born out of a genuine love for pizza, combined with a desire to share that passion with our community

As a family-owned and operated restaurant, we infuse each pizza with the warmth and care only a family can provide

Our skilled pizzaiolos use traditional techniques and the finest ingredients to craft pizzas that embody authenticity and flavor.


Our menu takes you on a culinary journey through the diverse regions of Italy. Indulge in a variety of hand-tossed pizzas, from classic Margherita to bold and innovative combinations that will tantalize your taste buds. We source the freshest and highest-quality ingredients to ensure each pizza is a masterpiece of taste and texture. Accommodating various dietary preferences, we offer gluten-free and vegan options without compromising on taste.

Mediterranean Delight

Fresh baby spinach, sun-dried tomatoes, Kalamata olives, feta cheese, and a drizzle of balsamic glaze

Embark on a journey to the Mediterranean with this flavorful pizza. The combination of savory Kalamata olives, tangy feta cheese, and the sweetness of sun-dried tomatoes will transport your taste buds to the sunny shores of Greece

BBQ Chicken Bonanza

Tender pulled BBQ chicken, red onions, cilantro, and a blend of mozzarella and cheddar cheese

Satisfy your cravings with this mouthwatering BBQ chicken pizza. The smoky and savory chicken pairs perfectly with the sharpness of red onions and the freshness of cilantro, creating a delightful fusion of flavors

Mouthwatering Pesto Chicken Supreme

Grilled chicken strips, sun-dried tomatoes, roasted garlic, artichoke hearts, and a generous spread of basil pesto sauce

Prepare to delight your taste buds with our Pesto Chicken Supreme pizza. The tender grilled chicken pairs perfectly with the tangy sun-dried tomatoes and the savory roasted garlic. Artichoke hearts add a delightful twist, while the luscious basil pesto sauce ties all the flavors together, creating a truly satisfying and savory experience

Vegetarian Garden Feast

Colorful bell peppers, zucchini ribbons, cherry tomatoes, black olives, and a blend of mozzarella and Parmesan cheese

For our vegetarian friends, we present this delightful garden feast pizza. Packed with fresh and vibrant vegetables, this pizza celebrates the goodness of nature. The combination of bell peppers, zucchini, and cherry tomatoes provides a burst of flavors that will leave you feeling nourished and satisfied
